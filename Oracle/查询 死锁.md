**查询当前的死锁：**

```sql
SELECT T2.USERNAME,
       T3.OBJECT_NAME,
       T2.OSUSER,
       T2.SID,
       T2.SERIAL#,
       T2.LOGON_TIME,
       T2.SQL_ID,
       T2.PREV_SQL_ID
  FROM V$LOCKED_OBJECT T1, V$SESSION T2, DBA_OBJECTS T3
 WHERE T1.SESSION_ID = T2.SID
   AND T1.OBJECT_ID = T3.OBJECT_ID
 ORDER BY T2.LOGON_TIME
```
